CeeBee Luxe

CeeBee Luxe represents our transition from a passionate, in-house studio creating meaningful interiors to a premium design firm creating custom, high-end living. What started out as a boutique project with a strong commitment to customized design has developed into a company renowned for its exquisite taste, outstanding craftsmanship, and classic beauty. Our journey reflects our dedication to pushing the boundaries of creativity while remaining grounded in Indian beauty, love, and honesty. CeeBee Luxe is the epitome of our design philosophy: elegant, expressive, and meticulously crafted.

Ms. Chitralekha Biswas, a first-generation female entrepreneur, started Cee Bee Design Studio with the goal of bringing together professionalism, uniqueness, and creative integrity in interior design. With the help of a skilled group of more than forty designers, project managers, and specialists, strong connect with skilled artisans and craftsmen, the studio has developed into a vibrant Private Limited Company that offers full design and execution services throughout India. Fundamentally, we think that a home is more than just a place; it is a narrative just waiting to be realized. We continue to transform ordinary life into meaningful, motivating, and engaging experiences with an uncompromising dedication to quality, transparency, and human-centric design.
