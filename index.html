Welcome to Cee Bee Design Studio, an Indian homegrown interior design brand where every home is a canvas and every design is a story. Founded by a first-generation woman entrepreneur, Cee Bee was created to bring together professionalism, personalisation, and creative integrity in interior design. What started as a boutique initiative has now evolved into a Private Limited Company with a strong team of 40+ designers, project managers, and professionals delivering exceptional design and execution services across India.
